About

Our school is the most prominent building in the town. It is a Christian convent school, and therefore it has a beautiful
Catholic Church. Our school has lush green and tall palm trees in the front and a beautiful garden. It also has a vast
playground where all the students gather to eat lunch and play different sports, such as cricket and football. I enjoy
lunchtime most when I meet my friends from other sections. Our school also has a massive auditorium with 500 seats.
It is famous for its annual function in the whole town. Our school is renowned for its many societies and clubs. I am a
member of the dance club and often perform on the stage of the auditorium. My favourite go-to place in the school
is the library. Our school library is very big. It has 25 shelves and thousands of books from every genre. I enjoy reading
encyclopaedias very much. I go to the library daily. We can also borrow the books we like from the library. Last year,
our school also won the “Best School Of The Town Award”. Everyone was very happy, and the principal distributed
sweets and candies among the children. It was an award well deserved.
